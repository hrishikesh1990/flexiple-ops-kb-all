# 16 — Performance Reviews

*Flexiple Operations Knowledge Base | Category D: Performance & Growth*

**Owner:** Hrishikesh | **Last Updated:** 2026-03-12

---

## Overview

Performance reviews are how we assess, develop, and make decisions about team members. This doc is for Senior OMs and Hrishikesh - it covers the review process, criteria, and decision frameworks.

---

## Review Cadence

| Review Type | Frequency | Participants |
|---|---|---|
| Monthly performance check | Monthly | Senior OM + each OM |
| Quarterly formal review | Quarterly | Senior OM + OM + Hrishikesh |
| Promotion consideration | As needed | Senior OM + Hrishikesh |
| PIP (Performance Improvement Plan) | As needed | Senior OM + Hrishikesh |

---

## Performance Rating Criteria

| Rating | Revenue vs Target | Qualitative Indicators |
|---|---|---|
| **B** (Below) | < 80% of target | Pipeline hygiene issues, missed deadlines, quality concerns |
| **M** (Meets) | 80-100% of target | Reliable execution, pipeline up-to-date, good communication |
| **M+** (Meets Plus) | 100-130% of target | Above-target consistently, mentoring others, client praise |
| **E** (Exceeds) | > 130% of target | Exceptional output, process improvement contributions, leadership |

---

## Review Process

### Monthly Check (Senior OM to OM)

1. Pull pipeline data and revenue numbers
2. Compare against target
3. Identify 1-2 strengths and 1-2 areas for improvement
4. Give direct, specific feedback
5. Document in the team tracker

### Quarterly Formal Review (Senior OM + OM + Hrishikesh)

1. Senior OM prepares a written review (performance data + qualitative assessment)
2. OM does a self-assessment
3. Three-way discussion: achievements, areas for growth, goals for next quarter
4. Rating assigned (B / M / M+ / E)
5. Promotion or PIP decisions made if applicable

---

## Promotion Decision Framework

| Level Transition | Key Criteria |
|---|---|
| L1 to L1.5 | 3+ months at M or above, solid pipeline discipline, initiative shown |
| L1.5 to L2 | 3+ months at M+ or above, client-ready communication, mentoring L1s |
| L2 to Senior OM | Sustained E-level performance, leadership qualities, Hrishikesh's assessment |

## Managing Underperformance

### Step 1: Direct Feedback (Week 1)
- Specific conversation about what's not working
- Clear expectations set for improvement

### Step 2: Coaching Plan (Week 2-4)
- Weekly check-ins with the Senior OM
- Specific, measurable improvement targets

### Step 3: PIP (If no improvement after 4 weeks)
- Formal 30-day Performance Improvement Plan
- Documented targets, weekly reviews, clear consequences

### Step 4: Decision (End of PIP)
- Continue (if improved) or exit (if not)
