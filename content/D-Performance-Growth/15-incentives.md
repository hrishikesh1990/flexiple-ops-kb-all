## Overview

Flexiple's incentive structure is designed to reward consistent performance. You earn based on placements you close, with higher payouts as you exceed targets.

## How Incentives Are Calculated

1. Revenue attributed to you = placements where you were the primary OM
2. Performance rating assigned based on monthly revenue vs target
3. Incentive payout = rating x applicable slab rate

---

## FAQs

**Q: What if a placement I closed gets cancelled within the first month?**
> A: Revenue is adjusted, and your incentive reflects the actual revenue realised.

**Q: How are shared placements handled?**
> A: If two OMs collaborate on a placement, the revenue is split as agreed with the Senior OM before the placement closes.
