# 12 — L2 Playbook

*Flexiple Operations Knowledge Base | Category C: Level Playbooks*

**Owner:** Hrishikesh | **Last Updated:** 2026-03-12

---

## Your Role as an OM-L2

You do everything an L1 does, plus you take on client-facing responsibilities and help mentor L1s in your pod. You're the bridge between execution (L1) and leadership (Senior OM).

---

## Core Responsibilities

| Area | What's Expected |
|---|---|
| **All L1 duties** | Sourcing, screening, pipeline - you still own these |
| **Client calls** | Lead client-facing calls (briefings, updates, feedback sessions) |
| **Interview coordination** | Manage scheduling between clients and candidates |
| **Mentoring** | Help 1-2 L1s in your pod improve their work quality |
| **Revenue** | Consistently hit targets; support pod revenue goals |

---

## What Changes from L1 to L2

| Dimension | L1 | L2 |
|---|---|---|
| Client interaction | Via Senior OM | Direct, with Senior OM oversight |
| Profile quality | Needs review | Self-sufficient, review-ready |
| Pipeline ownership | Own roles | Own roles + support pod pipeline health |
| Mentoring | Receive | Give (to L1s) |
| Escalations | Escalate to Senior OM | Handle some independently, escalate complex ones |

---

## Mentoring L1s - How To

- Review their profiles before they go to the client (catch errors, improve quality)
- Share your sourcing techniques and shortcuts
- Do joint calls - let L1s observe your client interactions
- Give specific, actionable feedback (not just "this needs improvement")
- Flag persistent issues to your Senior OM

---

## What "Good" Looks Like at L2

- Clients trust you to run calls independently
- L1s in your pod are improving because of your input
- Your pipeline management requires minimal Senior OM intervention
- You're consistently hitting targets

## What "Great" Looks Like at L2

- Clients specifically request to work with you
- You're mentoring L1s effectively - their metrics improve
- You identify process improvements and propose them
- You're ready for Senior OM consideration
