## 1. Welcome to Flexiple Operations

You are joining the engine that drives Flexiple's revenue. This guide will walk you through your first 30 days — from understanding the business to executing your first tasks independently.

**Your goal by Day 30:** Be able to independently source candidates, document profiles to quality standards, and manage interview scheduling for assigned roles.

## 2. Week 1: Foundation

**Objective:** Understand the business, the team, and the tools.

**Read these docs (in order):**

1. [[01-company-overview|Company & Business Model]]
2. [[02-team-structure|Team Structure & Roles]]
3. This doc

**Key activities:**

- Meet your Senior OM (your direct manager) and team members
- Get access to the Flexiple product — log in, explore the interface
- Shadow your Senior OM on 2-3 active roles to observe the workflow
- Understand the Talent Persona for at least 2 active roles
- Observe how candidates are sourced, reviewed, and moved through stages

**By end of Week 1, you should know:**

- What Flexiple does and how we make money
- Who is on your team and who you report to
- What the Flexiple product looks like and its key sections
- The basic flow: Source → Review → Interview → Client → Hire

## 3. Week 2: Core Processes

**Objective:** Learn the detailed process of sourcing and documentation.

**Read these docs:**

- [[05-sourcing-outreach|Sourcing & Candidate Outreach]]
- [[06-profile-documentation|Profile Documentation & Quality Standards]]

**Key activities:**

- Start sourcing candidates under supervision on 1-2 assigned roles
- Make your first candidate calls using the product's outreach module
- Practice documenting candidate profiles — resume, custom questions, internal view
- Get your first profiles reviewed by your Senior OM and incorporate feedback
- Learn the automation setup (WhatsApp, email, AI calling)

**By end of Week 2, you should be able to:**

- Navigate views and prioritize candidates
- Make candidate calls and extract relevant information
- Document a profile that passes Senior OM review on first attempt (target)

## 4. Weeks 3-4: Role Execution

**Objective:** Manage the full candidate lifecycle for assigned roles.

**Read these docs:**

- [[04-role-setup|Role Setup: Kick-off to Go-Live]]
- [[07-interview-management|Interview Management]]

**Key activities:**

- Own candidate sourcing for 2-3 roles
- Schedule Flexiple interviews and client interviews
- Follow the morning + 15-min-before interview follow-up routine
- Track candidate pipeline and update statuses
- Start hitting daily recommendation targets (aim for M-level: 6-7 recos/day)

**By end of Week 4, you should be able to:**

- Manage the sourcing-to-interview pipeline for assigned roles
- Follow the complete follow-up process without reminders
- Document profiles that need zero corrections from Senior OM

## 9. Common Mistakes to Avoid

- **Not following the view priority order** — always work top-down in the prioritized view
- **Sending profiles with incomplete data** — every field must be filled before moving to review
- **Skipping follow-up calls** — morning and 15-min-before calls are non-negotiable
- **Not extracting enough detail from candidate calls** — use the call transcript + GPT to fill all custom questions
- **Not asking for help** — your Senior OM is there to review and guide; flagging issues early is valued
